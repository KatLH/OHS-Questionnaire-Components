How Does the OrthoHealth System Work?

This program will help guide you to complete recovery, regardless of your starting position. The OrthoHealth System was designed to be safe, simple and effective - with you, being the center of every decision.

While the OrthoHealth System personalizes every decision based on your Digital Patient Profile, this app will deliver any activities, education, surveys or messages directly to your Action Center.

What are the symptoms of Degenerative Joint Disease?

[ .whiteTextBox Class Example ]

Symptoms may range from mild to disabling. A joint affected by DJD may be painful and inflamed. Without cartilage, bones rub directly against each other as the joint moves.

Pain, or a dull ache develops gradually over time. Pain is notably worse in the morning upon rising and initial movement(s).

[ .clearTextBox Class Example ]

Vigorous activity may cause pain to flare up. The joint may stiffen and look swollen or enlarged. Joint movements such as bending, straightening and rotation may become more difficult and loss of motion may follow.
